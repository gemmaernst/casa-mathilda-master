Casa Mathilda is a Boutique B&B  situated in Barcelona Eixample Area, a Smart neighbourhood and close to La Pedrera and La Sagrada Familia. The hotel is located on the main floor of a stately building which used to belong to one of the most prominent Catalan textile-manufacturing families in the early 20th century. It has been renovated in 2014 in a modern-elegant and  light vintage style , with a first quality finishing details.

We take care of all details as Mathilda would do, for you to feel like being at your own home. Our breakfast

includes our traditional “Pa amb tomaquet” with Jam, cheese or “fuet” . Croissants , Small varied pastries. All type of coffee, Tea, Yoghourt, cereals, different type of bread from the famous bakery Baluard, butter and Jam. Fruit Juice and Fresh Fruit every day. If you need something special please ask us, we will try to provide any special request.

We also offer the option of complete booking, in which you can rent the entire house – All for you! *Subject to availability; email  or pone us with your inquiries or requirements.

Reception open from 08:30 to 20:30. Check- in at 15h. Check-out at 12:00h
